## Replible Attachment

<Playground>
  <RonpaStory 
    getAvatar={(author) => `https://www.gravatar.com/avatar/${author}`}
    uploadFile={() => ({
      path: 'https://google.com/robots.txt',
      uploadedAt: new Date(),
    })}
    editorMode={'attachment'}
    username="Jack"
    repliable
    onAction={console.log}
    story={createMockStory()}
  />
</Playground>
